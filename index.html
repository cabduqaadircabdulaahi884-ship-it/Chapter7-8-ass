About The Sidemen

The Sidemen are a group of 6 British YouTubers. They started in 2013.

The members are Harry, Simon, Tobi, Ethan, Vick and Josh.

They make videos every Sunday called Sidemen Sunday. They have over 73 million subscribers.

They also have a clothing brand called SDMN and a restaurant called Sides.

They do charity football matches every year. They have a podcast called The Sidecast..
